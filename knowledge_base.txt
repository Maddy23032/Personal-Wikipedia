Peravallur is a well-developed residential area located in the northern part of Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Historically, the area was known for its vast agricultural lands and serene environment. Its development into a residential hub began in the late 20th century.

The most famous landmark in Peravallur is the Agatheeswarar Temple, an ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple is renowned for its traditional Dravidian architecture and is a significant spiritual center for the local community. Many local festivals are celebrated here with great enthusiasm.

Peravallur is also known for its strong sense of community and several well-regarded schools. The area is home to Thiru Vi Ka Park, a popular spot for recreation and family outings, featuring a jogging track and a children's play area. The local cuisine reflects the rich traditions of Tamil Nadu, with many small eateries offering authentic dishes.
